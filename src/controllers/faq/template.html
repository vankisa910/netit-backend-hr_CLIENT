Why do companies publish the same job ads over a long period of time?

The typical reasons include: companies that are just entering the market or are actively expanding; lack of sufficient suitable candidates with specialized experience and knowledge; within companies with a large number of employees it is normal to regularly have employees changing their position.

What is and is not permitted in the job ads?

The job ad should include all information necessary for the candidates to apply. To ensure efficient and comfortable environment and prevent using the job ads for promotion of products or services,we do not allow that the candidates are required to join Facebook groups, etc. The companies can include their corporate website, as well other additional information in their company profile page. It is up to the company to decide whether to include a contact person and a telephone number in the job ad text.

Do other employers have access to my CV and contacts?

No. The employers can access only the applications they have received to their job ads.

Is the employer required to pay a fee to view my CV?

No, there is no such requirement. The employers have full access in real time to absolutely all applications they have received to their job ads, regardless of the job type.

Does the employer see the contact details I have provided?

Yes, for the job ads you have applied to, the employer has full access to the contact details you have provided (phone, email, etc.) and the available information in your CV.
